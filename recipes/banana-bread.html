Easy Banana Bread

A great use for your ripened bananas

Banana bread that will make you come back for seconds

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1.5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
  2. Grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  3. Combine bananas, sugar, egg, and butter together in a bowl.
  4. Mix flour and baking soda together in a separate bowl
  5. stir into banana mixture until batter is just mixed.
    Stir salt into batter. Pour batter into the prepared loaf pan.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in the center of the bread
    comes out clean, about 1 hour.
